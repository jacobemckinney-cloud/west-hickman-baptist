About Our Church

Connecting People to God and Each Other

Our Mission

West Hickman Baptist Church exists to connect people to God and each other through the gospel of Jesus Christ. We are committed to worshiping Christ, teaching God’s Word, making disciples, and serving our community with the hope of the gospel.

Our History

West Hickman Baptist Church has been serving Hickman, Kentucky, since 1910. Through generations of ministry, seasons of change, and even the loss of the church building in a 2013 fire, God has remained faithful. After meeting in temporary locations, the church rebuilt and continued its mission of connecting people to God and each other. Today, West Hickman Baptist Church remains committed to worshiping Christ, teaching God’s Word, making disciples, and serving the Hickman community with the hope of the gospel.
